Contractor & Businesses

General liability insurance (GL) is coverage that can protect you from a variety of claims including bodily injury, property damage, personal injury and others that can arise from your business operations.

Who needs general liability insurance?

General liability insurance is often combined with property insurance in a Business Owners Policy (BOP), but itâ€™s also available to many contractors as a stand-alone coverage through a General Liability Insurance. As a contractor or small business owner, you need some form of general liability protection to safeguard you from financial ruin!

A single accident could result in a lawsuit that you might not be able to handle. A great way to protect against this is to make sure you have liability coverage that matches your level of exposure or the worst case scenario to protect you from finical ruin. Some employers or clients might also require you to carry a certain amount of general liability before you can work for them.
